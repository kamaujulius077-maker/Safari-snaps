About Me

The story behind the lens

Julius Kamau

Wildlife Photographer & Safari Guide

With over 10 years of experience photographing Kenya's wildlife, I've dedicated my career to capturing the raw beauty and intimate moments of Africa's most iconic animals in their natural habitat.

Based in Nairobi, I lead photography tours throughout Kenya's premier wildlife destinations including the Maasai Mara, Amboseli National Park, Tsavo, and the Samburu region.

My Approach

I believe in ethical wildlife photography. I never bait or disturb animals for the perfect shot. Patience is key – sometimes waiting hours for that one magical moment. The result is authentic, breathtaking images that showcase nature as it truly is.
